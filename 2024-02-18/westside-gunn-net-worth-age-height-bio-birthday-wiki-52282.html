Westside Gunn Biography

Westside Gunn is one of the most popular and richest Rapper who was born on July 27, 1982 in Buffalo, New York, United States. Alvin Lamar Worthy (born July 27, 1982), known professionally as Westside Gunn, is an American rapper. He is the brother of fellow rapper Conway, with whom he forms the duo Hall N Nash, and the cousin of fellow Buffalo native rapper Benny the Butcher. In 2014, he founded the record label Griselda Records, as well as The Purist’s Daupe! Records.

On April 17, 2020, he released his third studio album, Pray for Paris. The album had features from artists such as Tyler, the Creator, Joey Bada$$ and Freddie Gibbs. The album was extremely well received by critics.
