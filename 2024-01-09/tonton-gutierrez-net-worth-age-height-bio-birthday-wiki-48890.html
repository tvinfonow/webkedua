Tonton Gutierrez Biography

Tonton Gutierrez is one of the most popular and richest TV Actor who was born on September 11, 1964 in Manila, Philippines. Film and television star most well known for bringing to life Governor Eduardo Buenavista in the hit television series Pangako Sa ‘Yo. Since that breakout role, he has scored recurring roles in Minsan lang kita iibigin, Aryana, and That’s My Amboy.

In the 2012 drama series Aryana, he starred opposite Ella Cruz.

He is the son of legendary Filipino actors Eddie Gutierrez and Liza Lorena. Because his parents never married, he has eight half siblings from his parents’ other relationships. He married singer and actress Glydel Mercado in 2004.
