  • Tompall Glaser was an American country singer born in 1933.
  • He was a member of the influential outlaw country movement.
  • Glaser co-founded the famous Nashville music publishing company, “Hillbilly Central.”
  • His hit song “Put Another Log on the Fire” reached #21 on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart.
  • Glaser’s album “The Great Tompall and His Outlaw Band” peaked at #13 on the Billboard Top Country Albums chart.

  • He passed away in 2013 at the age of 79.
