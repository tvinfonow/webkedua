Top Facts about Rajesh Khanna

  • Rajesh Khanna was a Bollywood actor from 1966-1991.
  • He starred in over 160 films and won numerous awards.
  • Khanna had 15 consecutive solo hit films from 1969-1971.
  • He was known as the “first superstar” of Indian cinema.
  • Khanna’s famous dialogue: “Pushpa, I hate tears” (Amar Prem).
  • He married actress Dimple Kapadia in 1973 and had two daughters.
  • Khanna served as a Member of Parliament from 1992-1996.
  • In his later years, he battled cancer before passing away in 2012.
  • Fans still visit his Mumbai home to pay tribute to him.
  • His legacy continues to inspire new generations of actors and filmmakers.
