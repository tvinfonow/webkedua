What is John MacArthur's theology?

Authority of scripture Central to MacArthur’s theology is a very high view of scripture (the 66 books of the Protestant Bible). He believes that scripture is inerrant and infallible because it is “the Word of God” and that God cannot lie.

What denomination is the MacArthur Study Bible?

The MacArthur Study Bible, first issued in 1997 by current HarperCollins brand W Publishing, is a study Bible edited by evangelical Calvinist preacher John F. MacArthur with introductions and annotations to the 66 books of the Protestant Bible.
