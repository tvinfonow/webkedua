Top Facts about Frank Zane

  • Frank Zane is a retired American professional bodybuilder and author.
  • He is a three-time Mr. Olympia and his physique is considered one of the greatest in the history of bodybuilding.
  • He started bodybuilding as a teenager and was inducted into the IFBB Hall of Fame in 1999.
  • He has a Bachelor of Science degree from Wilkes University and a master’s degree in experimental psychology from California State University, San Bernardino.
  • He famously trained with light weights, but found the size necessary for success at the Olympia by training with heavier weights.
  • He is one of only three men to have defeated Arnold Schwarzenegger in a bodybuilding contest.
  • He competed for over 20 years and won Mr. America, Mr. Universe, Mr. World, and Mr. Olympia during his career.
  • He famously owned and operated “Zane Haven” in Palm Springs, California.
  • He has written a newsletter entitled Frank Zane Newsletter since 1998.
  • He received the Arnold Schwarzenegger Lifetime Achievement Award in 2003.
  • He appeared in the movie “See Arnold Run” and the Tribeca Festival documentary Challenging Impossibility in 2011.
  • He runs his own website and appears at seminars and book signings.
  • He is filming a feature documentary on his life in June 2020.
