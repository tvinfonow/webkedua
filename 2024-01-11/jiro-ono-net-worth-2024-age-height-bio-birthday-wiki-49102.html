Jiro Ono Biography

Jiro Ono is one of the most popular and richest Chef who was born on October 27, 1925 in Japan, Japan. Japanese chef best known as the owner of Sukiyabashi Jiro, the renowned Japanese sushi restaurant in Ginza, Tokyo, Japan. He is the subject of the award-winning 2011 documentary Jiro Dreams of Sushi.

He has cooked sushi for President Barack Obama, who called Jiro’s sushi the best he had ever eaten.

His sons, Yoshikazu Ono and Takashi Ono, are also sushi chefs.
