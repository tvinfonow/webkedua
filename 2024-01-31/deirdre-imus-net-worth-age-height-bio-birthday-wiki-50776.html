Deirdre Imus is one of the most popular and richest Writer who was born on August 4, 1964 in New York, United States. Deirdre Coleman Imus (born August 4 1964) is an American author as well as a health advocate and radio personality . She is also the president and founder of the Deirdre Imus Environmental Health Center which is part of Hackensack University Medical Center (HUMC) located in New Jersey, United States. She is also co-founder and director of the Imus Cattle Ranch for Kids suffering from Cancer and is her books include: The Greening of Your Cleaning and The Imus Ranch cooking with Kids as well as Cowboys.

The Deirdre Imus Environmental Health Center studies the potential connection between different diseases and environmental hazards. The center was awarded”Quality New Jersey’s Environmental Award “Quality New Jersey’s Environmental Award” on May 8, 2006.

Deirdre Imus has expressed concern over the chemicals used in commercial cleaning products and her book Greening Your Cleaning, published in April 2007, advocates the use of alternative cleaning products. Greening Your Cleaning contends that chemicals such as ammonia are harmful and she calls for “environmentally responsible” ways to clean around the house. She appeared on the NBC Today Show on April 6, 2007, to demonstrate the cleaning methods discussed in her book. A scheduled book tour was postponed, however, in the wake of the controversy surrounding the cancellation of the Imus in the Morning show.

On May 7, 2007, she was named to the board of directors of the National Autism Association. and on May 22, 2007, the National Audubon Society announced that Deirdre Imus was one of four women to receive the Society’s annual Rachel Carson Award for Women in Conservation. In making the announcement, the Audubon Society cited her as “a pioneer inspiring parents and schools to clean using non-toxic products … [persuading] more than 200 institutions nationwide to switch to green cleaning products and her work has led directly to three ‘Green Cleaning’ executive orders that will vastly improve the health of millions of children.”

The year 1999 was the time Don Imus and Deirdre created in 1999 the Imus Cattle Ranch for Kids with Cancer, a working cattle ranch located near Ribera, New Mexico, 50 miles east from Santa Fe. This ranch is non-profit foundation for children suffering from cancer and their the siblings who are SIDS victims. The Ranch has three homes, one located in Manhattan and another located in Westport, Connecticut, and one located in Ribera, New Mexico.
