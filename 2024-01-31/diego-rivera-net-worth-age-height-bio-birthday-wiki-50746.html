Top Facts about Diego Rivera

  • Diego Rivera was a prominent Mexican muralist.
  • He was born on December 8, 1886, in Guanajuato, Mexico.
  • Rivera studied art in Europe and later returned to Mexico.
  • His murals depict social and political themes of Mexican culture.
  • He married fellow artist Frida Kahlo in 1929.
  • Rivera’s most famous works include the Detroit Industry Murals and Man at the Crossroads.
  • He had a tumultuous relationship with the Mexican government due to his communist beliefs.
  • Rivera died on November 24, 1957, in Mexico City at age 70.
  • His legacy continues to inspire artists around the world today.
  • The Diego Rivera Museum is located in his childhood home in Guanajuato.
