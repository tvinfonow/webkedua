E. G. Daily is one of the most popular and richest Actress who was born on September 11, 1961 in Los Angeles, California, United States.

Besides doing voice-overs, Daily has acted in film and on TV. She played Sandy Burns on the PBS series The Righteous Apples from 1980 to 1981. She also appeared on such shows as Laverne & Shirley, CHiPs, Fame and Friends. She was also in the video for Rod Stewart’s song “Young Turks” as Patti, one of the runaway teens.
