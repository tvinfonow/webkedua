Mala Powers Biography

Mala Powers is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actress who was born on December 20, 1931 in San Francisco, California, United States. American actress, who was awarded a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in recognition of her roles in films such as City by the Sea and City That Never Sleeps. She also had more than 100 television credits, including Bonanza as well as Bewitched.
