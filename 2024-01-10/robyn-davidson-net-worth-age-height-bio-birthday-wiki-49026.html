Davidson left Alice Springs in 1977 to travel west with a dog, four camels, Dookie (a large male), Bub, Zeleika (a wild male), and Goliath (Zeleika’s offspring). Although she had not intended to write about the trip, she eventually agreed to write an article in National Geographic. After meeting Rick Smolan, a photographer from Alice Springs, she insists that he be the photographer of the entire journey. Smolan, who she shared a “on-again, off-again” relationship with during the trip, drove three times to meet her during the nine-month journey.
