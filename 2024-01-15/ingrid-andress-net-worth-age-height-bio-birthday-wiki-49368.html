Ingrid Andress Biography

Ingrid Andress is one of the most popular and richest Country Singer who was born on September 21, 1991 in Highlands Ranch, Colorado, United States. Country music singer and songwriter whose 2019 single “More Hearts Than Mine” found it’s way onto Billboard’s Hot Country Songs chart. She was signed to Warner Nashville records.

She has written songs for Sam Hunt and Alicia Keys.

Andress ascended to No. 1 on Billboard’s Emerging Artists chart in April 2020 which is due to the chart arrival of her debut studio album, Lady Like, and the rise of her single “More Hearts Than Mine”. Her debut single, More Hearts Than Mine, peaked at number one on the Mediabase Country Chart.

Andress signed with Warner Nashville in mid 2018. In 2019, she debuted with the single “More Hearts Than Mine”. The song has charted on the Billboard Hot Country Songs and Country Airplay charts. Andress cites Faith Hill and the Dixie Chicks as her musical inspirations, and notes that she always wants to write her own songs unless she finds an exceptional song written by someone else. On March 27, 2020, she released her debut studio album Lady Like through Warner Music Nashville.
