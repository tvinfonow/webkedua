What was Bobby Seale convicted of?

Seale attended Berkeley High School, then dropped out and joined the United States Air Force in 1955. Three years later, a court martial convicted him of fighting with a commanding officer at Ellsworth Air Force Base in South Dakota, resulting in a bad conduct discharge.

What did Bobby Seale do after the Black Panther Party?

After the decline of the Black Panther Party, Seale taught in the Afro-American Studies Department at Temple University in Philadelphia. He moved back to Oakland in 2002 to help young activists bring about social change.
