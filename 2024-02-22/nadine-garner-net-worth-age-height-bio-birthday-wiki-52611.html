Nadine Garner Biography

Nadine Garner is one of the most popular and richest Actor who was born on December 14, 1970 in Knoxfield, Australia. Nadine Lynette (born 14/12/70 in Knoxfield, Melbourne) was an Australian actress who began her career as a teenager performer.

In 1991/92, Garner was Arlene Toomer on the BBC comedy series Boys From The Bush. Since then, she has had a number of guest and minor roles in a variety of British and Australian television series including Prisoner and My Brother Tom miniseries and two series of Boys From The Bush for 1991/92.

Garner was married to Cameron Barnett, and they have two sons: Eden (born 2006) and Jem. In 2018 she revealed that she and Barnett were divorced. She grew up in the Melbourne suburb of Knoxfield.

Garner played Detective Senior Constable Jennifer Mapplethorpe in the police drama series City Homicide, which debuted in 2007. Starting in February 2013, she appeared as Jean Beazley, the housekeeper, in The Doctor Blake Mysteries with Craig McLachlan.

Garner’s film debut was in The Still Point (1986), and she also appeared in Bushfire Moon (87), Mull (88), Fresh Air (1999), Fresh Air (1994), Fresh Air (2006), The Book of Revelation (2006) and Razzle Dazzle: An Adventure into Dance (2007).
