Robert S. Tucker Biography

Robert S. Tucker is one of the most popular and richest Businessperson who was born on March 16, 1970 in New York, United States. Robert S. Tucker, born March 16, 1970, is an American businessman. T&M Protection Resources is his company, which provides security, intelligence, and investigative services. He is the chairman and CEO. He is Richard Tucker’s grandson. He lives in Scarsdale (New York).

Tucker was elected president for two years and chairman of ALDONYS (Associated Licensed Detectives of New York State), which promotes the interests of licensed detectives. On February 26, 2009, he was elected to the position.

In 2012 Tucker divested T&M’s U.S. security service business to Universal Protective Services of Orange, CA and its explosive detection canine operation to MSA Security of New York City with both transactions at record purchase multiples. In 2014, the Appellate Division of the Second Judicial Department of the Supreme Court of the State of New York appointed Tucker a member of the Committee on Character and Fitness for the Ninth Judicial District.

On November 30, 2015, Tucker received the Distinguished Service Award from the Citizens Crime Commission of New York. The award was presented by the Commission’s President, Richard Aborn. The Distinguished Service Award recognizes individuals who have demonstrated an ongoing commitment to criminal justice innovation.

Pegasus and Tucker began a plan in 2008 to jointly invest in acquisitions in Israel of security companies. This has led T&M Holding Israel Ltd to become one of the most important security and janitorial services companies in Israel with more than 7,000 employees.
