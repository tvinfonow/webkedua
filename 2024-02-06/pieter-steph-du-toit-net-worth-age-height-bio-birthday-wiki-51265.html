Pieter-Steph du Toit Biography

Pieter-Steph du Toit is one of the most popular and richest Rugby Union Player who was born on August 20, 1992 in Cape Town, Western Cape, South Africa. Pieter Stephanus du Toit, a South African professional rugby league player, was born 20 August 1992. Du Toit is a Super Rugby player who plays either as a flanker or a lock for both the South Africa national rugby team and the Stormers. He was named the 2019 Men’s World Rugby Player Of The Year after he won the Rugby World Cup with South Africa.

Du Toit, the grandson and older brother to Johan, a professional rugby player, is Piet “Spiere”, a former Springbok prop. Both brothers were signed to the Sharks in 2014 and 2015. They then reunited at the Stormers in 2017.

Du Toit was named in South Africa’s squad for the 2019 Rugby World Cup. South Africa won the tournament, defeating England in the final. He was awarded the World Rugby Men’s 15s Player of the Year in 2019.

Du Toit signed a two year extension to his contract with the Sharks in July 2013. Du Toit did sign a second contract renewal, and the Sharks announced his resignation in October 2015.
