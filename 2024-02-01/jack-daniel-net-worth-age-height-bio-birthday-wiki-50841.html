Jack Daniel Biography

Jack Daniel is one of the most popular and richest Entrepreneur who was born on September 5, 1850 in Lynchburg, Tennessee, United States. American businessman best known for distilling his own line of Tennessee whiskey, which later expanded into the famed Jack Daniel’s brand.

His company sponsored the Richard Childress car in the NASCAR Sprint Cup, which had Dale Earnhardt as one of the drivers.

He was born the youngest of 10 children. His parents were Calaway and Lucinda Daniel. He was close to his nephew Lem Motlow and gave him the distillery in 1907.
