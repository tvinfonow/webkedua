Elyse Sewell Biography

Elyse Sewell is one of the most popular and richest Model who was born on June 10, 1982 in Albuquerque, New Mexico, United States. The first time she was famous was her third-place finish on the very first season of the American show Next Top Model, she was able to explore a modeling career internationally and had a particularly successful run with her work in Hong Kong, appearing in an advertisement commercial in the name of Chow Sang Sang Jewelry and being featured on the front cover of Hong Kong edition of Harper’s Bazaar.

She was ultimately eliminated as the title of America’s Next Top Model to Illinois-born model She ultimately lost America’s Next Top Model to Illinois-born model Adrianne Curry..

In 2004, Sewell started a LiveJournal blog about her experiences of modeling overseas. She published a book called Beauty and The Biz: The International Adventures of America’s Third-to-Next Top Model, in Hong Kong in 2006 based in part on her (now abandoned) blog. She appeared at multiple book signings across Hong Kong. Her other writing credits include a satirical piece for Bust Magazine on how to be a reality TV star, as well as posts for the website Lemondrop.

In 2005, one of her “confessional” clips from the show, in which she expressed a profanity-laden rant against the people on the show, earned her 16th place on E!’s Most Outrageous TV Moments. She attributes this outburst to the unusual requirement on the show to voice one’s private thoughts and to soliloquize in a “confessional booth”.

She grew up living in Albuquerque, New Mexico. She was at some point, romantically linked in a relationship with The Shins member Martin Crandall.
