Nina Mae McKinney Biography

Nina Mae McKinney is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actress who was born on June 13, 1912 in Lancaster, South Carolina, United States. She was one of the first African-American film stars in the United States and England and starred in the 1929 film Hallelujah!

She co-starred with actor Paul Robeson in the 1935 film Sanders of the River.

She was married to Jazz musician, Jimmy Monroe from 1935 to 1938.

What was Nina Mae McKinney known for?
