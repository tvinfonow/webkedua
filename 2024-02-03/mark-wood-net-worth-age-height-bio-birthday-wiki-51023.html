Mark Wood Biography

Mark Wood is one of the most popular and richest Association Football Player who was born on June 27, 1972 in Ashington, England, United Kingdom. Gregory Mark Wood CBE (born 1953 in County Durham) has been in charge of numerous tech and financial services startups, both in the UK as well as New York City.

Chairman of Digitalis that specializes on Online Reputation Management; and chairman of the audit committee and non-executive director for the RAC Limited. He was previously chair of Beta London Advertising and Lloyds Insurer Chaucer PLC. He was also the Jardine CEO from 2011 to 2014. Lloyd Thompson Employee Benefits between the year 2011 until 2014. He is a regular contributor in the media about the subject of pensions as well as insurance. Wood is also the chairman of PensionBee Limited, Acquis Insurance, Utility Bidder, and Leakbot.

Wood is the former chairman of the NSPCC (National Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Children), serving for nine years until he left the role in October 2019. He also ran the Full Stop campaign to raise £280m through events such as a dinner with Bill Clinton and Elton John in St Petersburg.

In 2010 Wood earned an honorary degree in management at Anglia Ruskin University.
