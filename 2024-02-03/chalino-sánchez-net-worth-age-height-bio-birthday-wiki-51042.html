Top Facts about Chalino Sánchez

  • Chalino Sánchez was born in Sinaloa, Mexico in 1960.
  • He began singing and composing music at a young age.
  • His brother’s murder inspired him to write his first corrido.
  • He recorded over 90 albums during his career.
  • Chalino became known for his unique style of corridos that told stories of real-life events and people.
  • He was shot and killed after a concert in Culiacán, Mexico in 1992.
  • His death only increased his popularity among fans of regional Mexican music.
  • Many artists have covered his songs since his death, including Los Tigres del Norte and Jenni Rivera.
  • A biographical film about Chalino’s life was released in 2007 called “Chalino Sanchez: Una Historia Verdadera.”
  • He is considered one of the most influential figures in the history of regional Mexican music.
