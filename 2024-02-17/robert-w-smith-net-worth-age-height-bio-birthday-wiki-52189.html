Robert W Smith Biography

Robert W Smith is one of the most popular and richest Composer who was born on October 24, 1958 in Alabama, United States. He is best known for his compositions for band, drum & bugle corps, and symphonic orchestra. In the first forty years of his career, he published nearly 1,000 musical works. Earhart: Sounds of Courage and The Sound and the Fury are his most famous compositions. He also composed symphonies that honor the literary works of Dante, Homer and Cervantes.

He was born the same year that fellow American composer Anthony Braker.

He was raised in Daleville (Alabama). Susan Smith, his wife, was a bandleader.
