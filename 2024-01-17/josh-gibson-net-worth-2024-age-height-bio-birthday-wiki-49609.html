Josh Gibson Biography

Josh Gibson is one of the most popular and richest Baseball Player who was born on December 21, 1911 in Buena Vista, United States. The ‘Black Babe Ruth‘ was an unmatched power hitter in the Negro Leagues; tragically died three months before baseball was integrated.

He followed his good friend Satchel Paige as the second Negro Leagues player to be inducted into the Baseball Hall of Fame.
