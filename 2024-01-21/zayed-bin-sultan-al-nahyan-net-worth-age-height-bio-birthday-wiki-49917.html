Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan Biography

Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan is one of the most popular and richest World Leader who was born on May 6, 1918 in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. First president of the United Arab Emirates. He was an integral part of the creation of the UAE from 1966 to his death in 2004.

Both he and Prime Minister Mohammed Binrashid Al Maktoum held positions of power in the United Arab Emirates.

Six wives and 29 children were his, including Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan. He was also the son of Sheikha salma bint Butti (Abu Dhabi ruler) and Sheikh Sultan bin Khalifah Al Nahyan. Heikh Shakhbut bin Sultan Al Nahyan was his oldest brother. His grandfather, Sheikh Zayed bin Khalifa Al Nahyan was also a ruler.
