Morten Harket Biography

Morten Harket is one of the most popular and richest Pop Singer who was born on September 14, 1959 in Kongsberg, Norway. He is a new wave artist, best known for being the frontman of synth-pop group A-ha. They are best known for their 1985 hit, “Take on Me”. He performed with Soldier Blue.

He has appeared with Ben Miller on The Armstrong and Miller Show.

Born in Kongsberg, Norway, Harket began his musical career in 1977 as the frontman of the progressive rock band Savoy. He first came to prominence as a member of A-ha in 1982, with whom he achieved global success in the 1980s and 1990s. The band’s ninth and final studio album, Foot of the Mountain, was released in 2009.

Harket has also appeared as an actor and voice actor on several occasions. His first film role was in the Norwegian film Kjærlighet på pinne (1979). He also had a small role in the 1984 film Splash. In 2006, he provided the voice of the character Håkon in the Norwegian-language version of the Disney film Cars.

From 1989 to 1998, he was married to Camilla Malmquist-Harket. Later, he had affairs with Anne Mette Undlien (now Inez Andersson). He is the father of three children: two sons and one daughter.
