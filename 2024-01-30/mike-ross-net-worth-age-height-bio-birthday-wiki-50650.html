Mike Ross Biography

Mike Ross is one of the most popular and richest Politician who was born on August 2, 1961 in Texarkana, Arkansas, United States. American Democratic politician who represented Arkansas in the U.S. Congress. From 1990-2000, he also served in the Arkansas State Senate.

When he was twenty years old, he worked on the re-election campaign for then-Governor of Arkansas Bill Clinton.

He and his wife, Holly, owned a pharmacy together until they sold the business in 2007. They raised two children.
