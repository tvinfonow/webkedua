Sanju Viswanath Samson (born 11 November 1994) is an Indian cricketer who represents Rajasthan Royals in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and the Indian national cricket team. A wicket-keeper/right-handed batsman, he made his Twenty20 International debut for India against Zimbabwe on 19 July 2015. He is the youngest batsman to score 1,000 runs in the IPL and the youngest cricketer to captain a Ranji trophy side.

He made his debut for the Kerala Ranji Trophy team on 3 November 2011. He was the vice-captain of under 19 Indian team.
