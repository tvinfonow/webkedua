What does aka mean?

also known as — used to indicate another name that a person or thing has or uses.

Which is correct AKA or aka?

Both forms are popular, but most dictionaries and style guides prefer aka, so you should, too. In short, Use aka.

How do I use aka?

Replace “also known as” with AKA. This is the best way to tell if you are using AKA correctly. For example, in the phrase “Jennifer Jones, AKA my best friend” adding “also known as” in place of “AKA” would make it, “Jennifer Jones, also known as my best friend.” This still makes sense, so AKA works well here.
