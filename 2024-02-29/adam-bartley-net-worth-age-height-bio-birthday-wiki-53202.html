Adam Bartley Biography

Adam Bartley is one of the most popular and richest TV Actor who was born on January 28, 1979 in Minnesota, Minnesota, United States. Notable for his role as Deputy Ferguson, a.k.a. ‘The Ferg,’ on the television crime series Longmire, Bartley also made guest appearances on Bones, Vegas, Criminal Minds, and NCIS Los Angeles.

He appeared with Ethan Embry in the 2014 comedic action film Armed Response.

Bartley was born in Minnesota, and is a 1997 graduate of Eden Prairie High School in Eden Prairie. In 2001, Bartley graduated from Southern Methodist University with a bachelor of fine arts degree in acting. While in college, he worked at Reunion Arena during Dallas Mavericks basketball games.

Bartley’s film roles include parts in 2013’s Armed Response and 2017’s Annabelle: Creation.

He spent his childhood in Minnesota and, before launching his screen acting career in Los Angeles, spent time in Texas, Alaska, New York, and Illinois.
