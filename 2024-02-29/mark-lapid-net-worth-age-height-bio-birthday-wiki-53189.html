Mark Lapid is one of the most popular and richest Actor who was born on February 16, 1980 in Philippines. Mark Tadeo Lapid (born February 16th in 1980) is an actor and politician from the Philippines. He was the governor of Pampanga (2004-2007). The President Benigno Aquino III appointed him as chief operating officer for TIEZA. Tourism Infrastructure and Enterprise Zone Authority (TIEZA). TIEZA is an agency attached to within the Department of Tourism (DOT) which designs, regulates and oversees tourism enterprise zones that were created by Republic Act 9593.

Lapid has filed the CoC (Certificate of Candidacy) for Senator on October 14th on the 14th of October, 2015. Lapid will run under the Koalisyon ng daang Matuwid. However, he lost the race and finished 19th in the vote, ahead of his coalition partner, Sec. Jericho Petilla

He was tied to actor Tanya Garcia in a civil wedding on July 30th, 2010, at Marissa Hall in Porac, Pampanga. The couple has two children: Mischa Amidala (born on September 21 in 2007) as well as Matilda Anika (born October 29 in 2010) located in Las Vegas, Nevada.
