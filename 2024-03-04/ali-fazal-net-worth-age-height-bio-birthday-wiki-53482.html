Ali Fazal is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actor who was born on October 15, 1986 in India, India. After starring in the 2011 Bollywood feature Always Kabhi Kabhi, this Indian screen actor played major roles in Fukrey, 3 Idiots, and several other popular films.

In 2004, he participated in school plays and engaged in debates. He was selected for the role of Trinculo in the role play of The Tempest by William Shakespeare. Fazal went to Mumbai and graduated in economics from St. Xavier’s College.
