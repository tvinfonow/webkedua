Maktoum bin Mohammed Al Maktoum Biography

Maktoum bin Mohammed Al Maktoum is one of the most popular and richest Politician who was born on November 24, 1983 in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

Sheikh Maktoum bin Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum (Arabic: مكتوم بن محمد آل مكتوم ‎; born 24 November 1983) is the deputy ruler of Dubai, and the Chairman of Dubai Media Incorporated. He was named as the deputy ruler of Dubai in February 2008 when his elder brother Hamdan was made crown prince.

In 6 June 2019, he married Maryam Bint Butti Al Maktoum in a royal wedding at the Dubai World Trade Center.
