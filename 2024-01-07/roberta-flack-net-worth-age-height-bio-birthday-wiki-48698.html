Top Facts about Roberta Flack

  • Roberta Flack is an American singer born in 1937.
  • She won four Grammy Awards throughout her career.
  • Her hit song “Killing Me Softly With His Song” sold over one million copies.
  • Flack’s music combines elements of jazz, soul, and R&B.
  • She was the first African-American woman to win a Grammy for Record of the Year.
  • Flack has collaborated with other famous musicians such as Donny Hathaway and Peabo Bryson.
  • Her album “First Take” reached #1 on the Billboard charts in 1969.
  • In addition to singing, she also plays piano and guitar.
  • Flack has been inducted into the North Carolina Music Hall of Fame.
  • She continues to perform and record music today at age 84.
