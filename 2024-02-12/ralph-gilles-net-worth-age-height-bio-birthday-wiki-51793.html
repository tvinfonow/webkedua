Ralph Gilles Biography

Ralph Gilles is one of the most popular and richest Entrepreneur who was born on January 14, 1970 in New York City, New York City, United States. Ralph Victor Gilles (French pronunciation: ​[ʒij] ; born 14 January 1970) is a Haitian-Canadian-American automobile designer and executive. Gilles was the President and CEO of Chrysler’s SRT brand and Senior Vice President of Design at Chrysler before being promoted to Head of Design for Fiat Chrysler Automobiles in April 2015.

In August, 2008, Gilles replaced retiring Trevor Creed as Senior Vice President of Design. In October 2009, Gilles was promoted to President and CEO of the Dodge car brand. He was replaced as Dodge’s CEO in June 2011, remaining Senior Vice President of Design and becoming CEO of Chrysler’s Street and Racing Technology division.
