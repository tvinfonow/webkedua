Wendy Starland Biography

Wendy Starland is one of the most popular and richest Jazz Musician who was born on February 9, 1981 in New York City, United States. Starland is a lead vocalist on Moby’s album Last Night, which went to number two on the Billboard charts. She was also featured on Moby’s follow up album, Last Night Remixed. Wendy Starland is an American singer, songwriter and music producer from Los Angeles.
