Juan Orlando Hernández Biography

Juan Orlando Hernández is one of the most popular and richest World Leader who was born on October 28, 1968 in Gracias, Lempira Department, Honduras. He lost to Libre Party politician Xiomara Castro who was the wife of the former Honduran Vice President Manuel Zelaya, during the presidential election of 2013.
