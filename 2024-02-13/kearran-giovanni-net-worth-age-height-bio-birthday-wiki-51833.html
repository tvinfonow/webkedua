Kearran Giovanni Biography

Kearran Giovanni is one of the most popular and richest TV Actress who was born on December 16, 1981 in Lafayette, United States. Best known for her portrayal of Detective Amy Sykes in TNT’s Major Crimes in a series regular role, Giovanni has previously played a recurring part on the soap opera One Life to Live.

She starred alongside Raymond Cruz in Major Crimes.

Giovanni was born in Lafayette, Louisiana and raised in Katy, Texas. She attended The High School for the Performing and Visual Arts, and later University of Cincinnati – College-Conservatory of Music. In 2004, she moved to New York, and began her career on Broadway. She appeared in a number of productions, including Hugh Jackman: Back on Broadway, Catch Me If You Can, Guys and Dolls, Sweet Charity, and Tarzan.

In 2016, Giovanni had a recurring as Senator Diane Hunter in the ABC political drama series Designated Survivor. Following the conclusion of Major Crimes in 2018, Giovanni guest-starred on The CW series Dynasty and the CBS series Bull, before recurring on the second season of The CW’s Black Lightning as Cutter.

She was raised in Katy, Texas after being born in Lafayette, Louisiana. She and Philip Ambrosino have two children.
