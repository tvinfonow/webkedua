Win Morisaki Biography

Win Morisaki is one of the most popular and richest Actor who was born on August 20, 1990 in Myanmar. Win Morisaki (Sen Qi uin , Morisaki Win, Burmese Miuriichaakiiwng, born on August 20th 1990) is a Japanese actor and singer from Burmese descent. He is the vocalist in the lead of PrizmaX which is a J-pop boy group. He has appeared in movies and TV dramas in Japan and Myanmar and also in the Hollywood movie Ready Player One (2018).

He was named as a Myanmar ambassador for tourism in Myanmar.
