Mike Pierce Biography

Mike Pierce is one of the most popular and richest Mixed Martial Artist who was born on September 1, 1980 in Portland, Oregon, United States. Michael Pierce, born September 1, 1980, is an American retired mixed martial artist. He was a former Welterweight fighter in the UFC, and also participated in a single-fight stint with the WEC.

Pierce faced Paul Bradley at UFC on Fox 1 on November 12, 2011. This was a rematch of their time on regional circuit. He won via split decision.

For the third consecutive time Pierce fought a UFC newcomer in Kenny Robertson on February 5, 2011 at UFC 126. He won the fight via TKO due to punches early into the second round.

Pierce faced Seth Baczynski on December 15, 2012 at UFC on FX 6, replacing an injured Kyle Noke. He won the fight via unanimous decision.

Pierce was scheduled to face Josh Koscheck at UFC Fight Night 20 on January 11, 2010. Koscheck was defeated by Anthony Johnson at UFC 106. Pierce then faced Jon Fitch at UFC 107 on December 12, 2009. Pierce won the third round and almost finished Fitch after losing the first two rounds. He lost to Fitch via unanimous decision (29-28, 29-28, 29-28).
