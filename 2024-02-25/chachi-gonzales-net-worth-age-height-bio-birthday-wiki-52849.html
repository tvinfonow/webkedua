Hildén was born Olivia Irene Gonzales on January 23, 1996 in Houston, Texas. At the age of 6, Gonzales enrolled at a local dance studio and was trained as a ballet dancer. Gonzales became immediately intrigued and decided to participate in hip hop from then on. She enrolled at Lanier Middle School where hip hop was one of the dance styles taught. She was reserved at first, but after months of training Gonzales honed her skills and became more confident. Eventually, Gonzales’ dance teacher, impressed with Gonzales’ skill, asked her to lead the group she was being taught in.
