Duane Eddy Biography

Duane Eddy is one of the most popular and richest Guitarist who was born on April 26, 1938 in Corning, New York State, United States. Grammy Award-winning rock guitarist who used a twangy bass on “Rebel Rouser.” He sold more than twelve million records over the course of his career.

He and Ravi Shankar wrote the song “The Trembler” together.

He married Jessi Colter on February 3, 1962 and, after their divorce in 1968, he remarried Deed Eddy in 1969.
