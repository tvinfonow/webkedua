Bob May Biography

Bob May is one of the most popular and richest Golfer who was born on October 6, 1968 in Lynwood, Lynwood, United States. Robert Anthony May (born October 6, 1968) is an American professional golfer. He lost to Tiger Woods in a three-hole playoff for the 2000 PGA Championship at Valhalla.

From 2008 through 2010, May played primarily on the Nationwide Tour (now the Korn Ferry Tour) along with some PGA Tour events. May lost his status on the minor-league tour after missing 15 of 25 cuts in 2010; he played in only eight tournaments in 2011 and just twice in 2012.

May was in the top 50 of the Official World Golf Ranking for much of 2000 and 2001.
