Tom Liddy Biography

Tom Liddy is one of the most popular and richest Attorney who was born on July 20, 1962 in United States.

Liddy graduated from the St. Albans School in Washington, D.C in 1981. Later, he received a B.A. degree in History at Fordham University in 1986, the same college his two brothers, father and grandfather attended. He spent his junior year abroad in Jakarta, Indonesia, where he studied Economics and Theology and learned to speak Indonesian. After serving in the United States Marine Corps, he returned to Fordham and graduated with a J.D. degree in 1992.
