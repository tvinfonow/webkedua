Eleanor Burns Biography

Eleanor Burns is one of the most popular and richest Celebrity who was born on July 3, 1945 in Pennsylvania, Pennsylvania, United States. Known for hosting the Quilt in a Day television program and co-designing the Eleanor’s Anniversary Florals fabric collection, she also published more than a hundred books on the topic of quilting.

She and Marie Webster were both prominent American quilters and crafts writers.

A native of Zelienople, Pennsylvania, she eventually opened quilting stores in San Marcos, California and Paducah, Kentucky. She raised two children.
