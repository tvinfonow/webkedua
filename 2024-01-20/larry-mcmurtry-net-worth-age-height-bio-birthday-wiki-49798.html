Larry McMurtry Biography

Larry McMurtry is one of the most popular and richest Screenwriter who was born on June 3, 1936 in Archer City, Texas, United States. An American fiction writer and screenwriter known for his Texas and Old West settings, he is most famous for his 1985 Pulitzer Prize-winning novel, Lonesome Dove. He also wrote Terms of Endearment, a 1975 novel that was adapted into an Academy Award-winning film.

His wife, Norma Faye Kesey, was once married to counterculture author Ken Kesey.

He was raised on a ranch near Archer City, Texas. In 2011, he married Norma Faye Kesey.
