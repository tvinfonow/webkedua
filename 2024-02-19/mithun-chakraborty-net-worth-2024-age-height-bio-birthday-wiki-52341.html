Mithun Chakraborty Biography

Mithun Chakraborty is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actor who was born on June 16, 1950 in Barisal, Bangladesh. He was a Bollywood actor and won the Filmfare Award as Best Supporting Actor for his role in Agneepath, a 1991 film. His role in Mrigayaa, a 1976 film, earned him his first National Film Award as Best Actor.

He starred alongside Abhishek Bchan in Guru, a 2007 film.

In 1979, he married Yogeeta Bali, and they had four children together: Mahakshay Rimoh Namashi, Namashi, and Dishani.
