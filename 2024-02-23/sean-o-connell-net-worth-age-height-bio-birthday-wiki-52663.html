Sean O’Connell (born September 2 1983) is a retired American professional mixed martial artist who recently participated within the Light Heavyweight division of the Professional Fighters League. Professionally competing since 2007, O’Connell has formerly competed in his own championships, the Ultimate Fighting Championship and Maximum Fighting Championship.

On the 18th of January in 2015, O’Connell was then matched against Matt Van Buren at UFC Fight Night 59.; He won via a third round TKO. Both of them were awarded Fight of the Night honors. The fight was then fought by Anthony Perosh on May 10 on the night of UFC Fight Night 65. He was victorious via TKO in the opening round.

O’Connell faced Ilir Latifi on January 17, 2016 at UFC Fight Night 81. He lost the fight by knockout in the first round.
