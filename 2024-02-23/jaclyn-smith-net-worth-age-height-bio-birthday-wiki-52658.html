Jaclyn Smith is an American actress and businesswoman, best known for her role as Kelly Garrett in the television series Charlie’s Angels (1976–1981), and as a spokeswoman for Kmart from 1992 to present. She was born Jaclyn Ellen Smith on October 26, 1945 in Houston, Texas, the daughter of Margaret Ellen (Mann) and Jack Smith, a businessman. Her mother was of Irish descent and her father had English, Scottish, and Irish ancestry. She was raised in a strict Southern Baptist home. Smith attended Trinity University in San Antonio, Texas, where she was a member of Chi Omega sorority. She graduated in 1967 with a Bachelor of Arts in fashion design and modeling. After college, she moved to New York City to pursue a career in modeling and acting.
