James Burrows Biography

James Burrows is one of the most popular and richest Director who was born on December 30, 1940 in Los Angeles, California, United States. Veteran television director with more than 50 TV pilots to his name who co-created the NBC series Cheers. He directed every episode of Will & Grace and is also notable for directing Taxi, Friends and Frasier, among numerous other classic sitcoms.

He directed John Mahoney in Frasier, and in a 1998 adaptation of the play The Man Who Came to Dinner.

He was married to Linda Solomon from 1981 to 1993 with whom he had three children. He later married Debbie Easton. He is the son of playwright and director Abe Burrows.
